Lovergirl Lyric Video

I used After Effects and Procreate to make this lyric music video. To me, this song feels like something I would write in my Notes app; deep, gut wrenching thoughts and feelings. I knew I wanted a pink color palette because to me everything about this song screams the feminine experience or at least something that many women can relate to to some degree at some point in their life. Pink is typically associated with femininity, I wanted to embrace that. When I was younger I hated pink because it was seen as “girly” and “girly” was typically associated with being “weak” and I hated that. As I have gotten older I have embraced this love for pink and am more confident about who I am and what it means to me to be a woman. So I embraced pink in this project and drew different hearts, eyes and fier to depict the song in the way that I interpret it.

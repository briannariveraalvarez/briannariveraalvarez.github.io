Hollywood Monsters

This is a movie remix with a couple of Hollywood's classic monsters: Frakenstieins Bride, Dracula, The Wolf Man, and The Creature from the Black Lagoon. I enjoy watching movies and I wanted to encapsulate the aesthetic and some of my favorite monsters from each movie. I used Blue Monday by New Order and slowed it down to give this remix a unique feel and aimed to make each cut not too predictable. I also had fun layering different clips on top of each other, kind of like a collage. I used Premier Pro for this project.

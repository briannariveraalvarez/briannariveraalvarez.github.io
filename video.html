Hollywood Monsters

This is a movie remix with a couple of Hollywood's classic monsters: Frakenstieins Bride, Dracula, The Wolf Man, and The Creature from the Black Lagoon. I enjoy watching movies and I wanted to encapsulate the aesthetic and some of my favorite monsters from each movie. I used Blue Monday by New Order and slowed it down to give this remix a unique feel and aimed to make each cut not too predictable. I also had fun layering different clips on top of each other, kind of like a collage. I used Premier Pro for this project.

Look at me

This is a re-edit of the music video Can't you see me? by TOMORROW X TOGETHER that was made on Premiere Pro. I took clips from the video and replaced the song with a different song that I believed held the chaos aspect of the original music video. I wanted to expand on the chaos so I replaced it with Cradles by Sub Urban. Welcome to my world of chaos.

Lovergirl Lyric Video

I used After Effects and Procreate to make this lyric music video. To me, this song feels like something I would write in my Notes app; deep, gut wrenching thoughts and feelings. I knew I wanted a pink color palette because to me everything about this song screams the feminine experience or at least something that many women can relate to to some degree at some point in their life. Pink is typically associated with femininity, I wanted to embrace that. When I was younger I hated pink because it was seen as “girly” and “girly” was typically associated with being “weak” and I hated that. As I have gotten older I have embraced this love for pink and am more confident about who I am and what it means to me to be a woman. So I embraced pink in this project and drew different hearts, eyes and fier to depict the song in the way that I interpret it.

Little Danny!

An animation project done on After Effects that portrays the main character, Little Danny, on one of his many adventures where he successfully gets through all the different floors, collecting coins and the key that unlocks the door to escape. He walks funny (it's his quirk) and he has huge eyes. Each floor introduces him into a new world where he must then find the next ladder to make his escape. When designing this in Illustrator, I knew I wanted every floor to be very different to the previous floor so I was very particular about the colors I used as well as the kind of design the floor would have.

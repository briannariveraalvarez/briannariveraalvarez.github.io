Free Palestine

This is a glitched series where I glitched three pictures: a picture of a little girl waiting for food, a man screaming and a protest. I specifically picked these pictures to depict the children and adults in Gaza as well as those who are standing with them in outside countries. I provided the original picture as well as the final glitched products, please use the slider to reveal them.

Glitching is not normal. It is a sign that something is wrong.

January 2024

After glitching the original picture, I knew I wanted the focus to stay on the little girl so I did my best to not distort her too much. I was able to distort the color on her pink sweater that made her a little more vibrant helping bring more attention to her.

Red and Green

The glitches are mostly red, green and black, which actually happen to be the colors of the Palestinian flag. I copied and pasted the code that gave me those colors and was very particular about where I glitched out.

Palestine

I wanted the final version to feel more intense and raw and I believe I was able to do that by completely changing the colors and glitching out the top of the photo. Red is tinting most of the picture which helps bring focus to the message on the protest signs, however I wanted the flag and the the countries name to jump out

About

Brianna Rivera Alvarez is a video editor and graphic designer. She's a second year in college majoring in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Brianna has been passionate about art for as long as she could remember but fell in love with digital art in high school through video production and editing.

Most of her work is inspired by music but it is also inspired by pop culture and major events around the world. When she struggles to find inspiration from the world around her, she turns to her imagination and finds enjoyment in designing and creating characters. My sister took this pic by the way!
